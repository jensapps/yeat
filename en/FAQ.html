How are restaurants being displayed on Yeat?

For optimal use it's important to enable your location services on your phone. On opening of the app you immediately get an overview of all restaurants that have a table available in your neighbourhood. If you have selected favorites, you can also consult this list. If your location facility is off, you can only consult the list of available tables alphabetically.

How to handle notifications?

you can set your preferences for notifications according to your needs. You can manage notifications in your phone at all times. They override the application's settings anyway. Given the last-minute nature of the application, we recommend that you leave your notifications for Yeat on so as not to miss any opportunities.

Do you want to get a table for one of the coming days in a restaurant that is usually full? then mark the desired restaurant(s) as your favorite(s), make sure your notifications are turned on in your phone. Then you will be notified of all your restaurants marked as favorites when they put a table online.

As a user, do I have to pay anything for using Yeat?

The application is completely free for the user. The user does accept the terms and conditions of Yeat, by using the application. Read here the general conditions.

what happens when a booked table does not show up?

The general terms and conditions, approved by the user, state that a user who has booked the table commits himself to take the table with the number of available people on the specified date and time. If the user does not show up without any form of communication, the restaurant owner reserves the right to ask the user for compensation for this. This is a decision of the restaurant owner himself and is done directly between user and restaurant owner.

How many tables can I put online at the same time?

As many as you wish.

How long does my table stay online?

When placing your table online, you must indicate until what time you want to make the table visible. When the table is booked, the user is notified that the table is no longer available. This message remains for a short time.

Can I take a table offline?

You can take a table offline at any time without any extra costs.

How do users get to see my restaurants?

When you register your restaurant for registration, you enter all your details yourself and you can also upload a few photos. Users can then find you, either by name, location or other filters such as Gault & Millau score or Michelin stars.

I'm the owner of another restaurant, do I need to make another account?

It's only possible for 1 account per business address.

I want an employee to manage Yeat. What should I do ?

Send us an email with the details and email address of your employee via the following Link.. When your employee downloads the application, we ensure that he can manage your restaurant with his email address.
